# Show Emote

Display your favorite emotes directly on your stream with fun animations!

### Features
- **Flexible Triggers**: Allow viewers to spawn emotes on your screen either via chat commands (like `!showemote`) or automatically from the first emote in any chat message.
- **Dynamic Animations**: Choose from multiple built-in animations: Fade In/Out, DVD Bounce, or a fun Throw physics effect.
- **Queue System**: Keep your screen clean by limiting the maximum number of simultaneous emotes. Extra emotes can be safely queued to appear once space frees up.
- **Extensive Emote Support**: Natively renders static and animated emotes from Twitch, 7TV, BetterTTV (BTTV), and FrankerFaceZ (FFZ).
- **Customizable**: Adjust the amount of time each emote stays on screen and precisely control their display size.
